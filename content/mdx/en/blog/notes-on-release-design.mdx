excerpt: Small decisions around covers, metadata, and release pages have bigger weight than they look.
---

Release pages should not feel like generic cards with a playlist under them.

For this site the better direction is:

- heavy cover presence
- minimal but readable metadata
- direct access to the tracklist and download actions
- links to outside platforms only when they actually exist

That keeps the page personal and still usable.
